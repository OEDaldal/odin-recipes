Pie Crust Cookies

Description

These delicous crusty and buttery cookies will feel like fall has come to your kitchen.

With the help of cinnamon you won't believe how great they will taste.


Ingredients

  • 1 premade pie crust
  • all purpose flour for dusting
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled
